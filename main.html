Goa – The Beach Paradise of India

Goa is a famous tourist destination known for its beautiful beaches, historical churches, and vibrant culture. It attracts visitors from all over the world because of its scenic beauty, water sports, and delicious seafood. Goa is also known for its peaceful environment and exciting nightlife.

Top 5 attractions

  1. Baga Beach
  2. Calangute Beach
  3. Fort AguadDudhsagar Waterfalls
  4. Basilica of Bom Jesush

Must-try foods

  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Prawn Balchao
  • Chicken Cafreal
  • Vindaloo
  • Bebinca
